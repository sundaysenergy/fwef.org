November event: Political Panel

Date: 
Nov 18 2009 - 11:30am - 1:30pm
Join the Forum of Women in the Environmental Field for a light dinner and a panel discussion featuring Minnesota women who successfully advocate for the environment. Panelists will discuss what you can do at any level to affect environmental change. Whether you are an elected official, a lobbyist or a grassroots activist there are opportunities to improve the environmental health of our community. There will be plenty of time for questions and answers.
A light dinner will be served.  

Panelists:
  • Representative Kate Knuth
  • Molly Schultz, Conservation Minnesota Public Affairs Director  and Minnesota Environmental Partnership Policy Director
  • Barb Thoman, Co-founder of Transit for Livable Communities
Schedule: 
  • 5:30 pm: Registration open, dinner available 
  • 6:00 pm-6:15 pm: FWEF annual elections
  • 6:15 pm-7:00 pm: Panelists remarks 
  • 7:00 pm-7:20 pm: Q & A
  • 7:20 pm-7:30 pm: closing remarks and announcement of new board members
Cost: $12 for members, $18 for non-members, $10 for students

Location: University of St. Thomas, Murray-Herrick Campus Center

Directions:  http://www.otug.org/location/mh155.html#directions

RSVP: By Monday, November 16, 2009.
Please add this event to your cart and complete the checkout process to confirm your registration. You may choose to pay by credit card or check on the checkout screen.
 
Getting there:
  • Parking: Ramp parking is available at $1.50/hour. http://www.stthomas.edu/psps/Parking/stpaul2Dparking.pdf
  • Bus to the event: Please consider taking public transit to the event. UST is located at the corner of Summit and Cleveland avenues in St. Paul and is reachable bus. You can find your bus connection there by entering that intersection into the Metro Transit online trip planner.
  • Carpool: If you are interested in carpooling, contact Suzanne D'Souza at suzdsouza@hotmail.com or 612-741-1365.
