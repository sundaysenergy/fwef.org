July Outdoor Tour Event (family friendly!)

Date: 
Jul 23 2008 - 12:30pm - 2:00pm
July Outdoor Event: Tour of the Ramsey-Washington Metro Watershed District Office and Grounds

Location: Ramsey-Washington Metro Watershed District Office

Address: 2665 Noel Drive, Little Canada, MN 55117

Bring the whole family out on the evening of Wednesday, July 23 to tour a facility that was designed inside and out to reflect the importance of appreciating and caring for the environment, consistent with the Watershed District’s goals. Learn about the construction and operation of the green features of this building, how elements of the grounds and facility were planned to draw attention to the natural world, and network with others working in the environmental field. The tour will include activities appropriate for youth ages 6 and up. A picnic dinner will be served outside, or inside if the weather is inclement.

**** FREE native seed packets to all attendees!!! ***


Schedule:

5:30 pm Registration

5:35 pm The first tour gets underway, with an additional tour departing as people arrive

6:15 pm Picnic dinner and networking as the first tour finishes

Costs: $10 for students, $12 for members, $18 for non-members.

Special deal: Bring a guest for only $8 more. You pay the full price and only $8 for each guest you bring.

RSVP: By Friday, July 18, 2008. Please add this event to your cart and complete the checkout process to confirm your registration. You may choose to pay by credit card through PayPal, mailing a check to the Forum at the address provided on the checkout screen, or bringing payment with you (pre-pay preferred).

Parking: The parking lot at the watershed office is available for those attending the event. Overflow parking is available across the street at the City of Little Canada city hall.

Bus to the Event: Please consider taking public transit to the event. You can find a bus route by using the Metro Transit online trip planner.
