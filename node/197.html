Be listed in the FWEF virtual directory!

The Forum of Women in the Environmental Field is creating a directory for those interested in networking with others in the environmental field.  Provide as little or as much information about yourself and connect to those with similar interests!

By being listed in our directory, you're willing to let people contact you to ask questions about your career and/or environmental interests.  You may request to be removed from the directory at any time or change the information you provide.  This directory is for you and hope you find it helpful!

Note:  We will NEVER give out your email address (or other information) to anyone not connected to FWEF.

Email Melissa Wenzel if you have any questions, comments or suggestions.
