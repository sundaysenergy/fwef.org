March Networking Event

Date: 
Mar 24 2010 - 12:30pm - 2:00pm

 

Networking in the Environmental Field!

Co-hosted by the Center for Global Environmental Education at Hamline University!

  • Meet one-on-one with our event sponsors!
  • See how their positions are related to the environment.
  • Learn about environmental jobs in a variety of business sectors. 
  • Bring your resume.  We can assist you with ideas!
  • Talk to people who have found jobs in the environmental field!
  • Network=networth=greater chance of finding a job=friends!
Location: Hamline University - Sorin A & B (Sorin Dining Hall)

$12 for students/members, $18 for non-members (register by March 22 to avoid paying a $2 late registration fee)

Light dinner/appetizers will be served.
Meet representatives from these organizations!
Ctr for Energy and the Environ. Historical Information Gatherers
Aveda Corporation EnPro Assessment Corp
Capitol Region Watershed District Asset Recovery Corporation
Braun Intertec Qwest Communications
Pace Analytical Services General Mills
Barr Engineering Assoc. Women Geoscientists

Bus or Carpool to the Event: Please consider taking public transit to the event. You can find a bus route by using the Metro Transit online trip planner.  If you are interested in carpooling, contact Jennifer Schmitt at jorg0206@umn.edu.

Help us spread the word about this event! Print off the poster below and hang it up in your favorite coffee shop, bookstore, co-op, whatever!
