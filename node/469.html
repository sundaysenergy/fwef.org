Date: 
Jan 26 2011 - 11:30am - 1:00pm

January Event:   Full Circle Superior - A Walk Around the World's Greatest Lake

Join the FWEF to hear from Kate Crowley about her historic hike around Lake Superior.  Kate and her husband, Mike Link, walked around Lake Superior during the summer of 2010.  It took them 145 days to travel 1,550 miles.  Not bad for a couple of grandparents!  Come and join us for this unique view of the lake and its ecological importance to Minnesota and surrounding states.  A light dinner is included.
Cost: $12 for members & students, $18 for non-members  

Location: REI at 750 W. American Blvd, Bloomington, MN  55420 (952-884-4315)

Public transport to our event:  Metro Transit bus routes 4, 542 and 576 run near REI.
Carpool to our event:  If you are interested in carpooling please contact our transit coordinator at jorg0206@umn.edu.
