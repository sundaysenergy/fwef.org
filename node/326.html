Become a member of the Forum of Women in the Environmental field (or renew!)

The Forum of Women in the Environmental Field is thankful for your support!

But we couldn't do it without you.  Over a dozen women volunteer their time to create interesting environmental/networking events, post job and volunteer opportunities, and create unique resource links of interest to you.  Every event provides local/organic food, the number one cost for the Forum.  Please show your support by becoming a member or renewing your membership today!  

Besides supporting an organization you care about, membership benefits include: 

  • a discount for each event
  • Additional networking opportunities
  • A cloth bag (recycled plastic) with the FWEF logo on it-ask for your "member" bag at the next FWEF event.

 Visit: http://fwef.org/membership for more information or to become a member/renew your membership, good through 12/31/2010.

FWEF membership has not changed
