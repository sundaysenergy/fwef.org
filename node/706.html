2013 Event Schedule and 2012 Event Summary

2012 was an amazing year for the Women’s Environmental Network Minnesota.  This 20th year we hosted six events, and changed our name and logo.  Thank you to the Board of the Women’s Environmental Network for planning and organizing these events and working on the name and logo change!  Thanks to all of you who attended our events and became or renewed your membership in our organization.  Thank you to our event speakers, sponsors and cohosts for working with us in 2012.  Mark your calendars for 2013:

 

2013 Event Schedule

 

January 23 – surprise – more information will come soon

April 3 – Annual Networking Event

May 18 – Local Food and Drink Event  (members only dinner, followed by a tour that is open to all)

July 20 (Saturday) – 20th Anniversary Gala with silent auction

September 25 – Sustainability in Business Event

November 20 – Members Only Dinner – Political Panel open to all

 

Give a one year membership for only $25!  Member are invited to attend two dinners per year that are open only to members.  This is a tremendous opportunity in a small setting which makes networking count more than ever.  Members also receive $6 off the cost for each event.  Click here to become a member or give a gift membership.  Memberships and events are open to both men and women.
