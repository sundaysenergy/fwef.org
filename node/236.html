Special April Event: Speed Networking!

Date: 
Apr 29 2009 - 1:30pm - 3:30pm

The Forum of Women in the Environmental Field is pleased to announce a special April Event: Speed Networking!

The Minnesota Association of for Women Geoscientists and the Forum of Women in the Environmental Field are co-organizing a FREE speed networking event.  Meet and greet a number of great people in TWO different environmental organizations!  FREE local/organic appetizers from Common Roots Cafe. 

This event is generously sponsored by the following organizations.  Check them out!

  • Historical Information Gatherers, Inc 
  • Aerotek
  • Kraemer Materials and Mining
  • Barr Engineering
  • National Center of Earth-Surface Dynamics
  • Association for Women Geoscientists, Minnesota Chapter

This list will be updated as more companies/organizations/individuals sponsor the event.  Thank you to all of our sponsors!

Cost of attending event: FREE!  Even though there is no cost for this event, registration is still required.  Register today!

***NOTE:  when registering, choose "check or money order".  Do not choose "Paypal."  Thanks! 

Agenda:
  • 6:30-6:45 pm: Informal Networking - Appetizers
  • 6:45-6:50 pm: Introduction and Logistics Overview
  • 6:50-8:20 pm: Individual Networking Introductions
  • 8:20-8:30 pm: Wrap Up and Informal Networking

During the "Individual Networking Introductions" section, each participant will go around the table and give a brief one minute introduction by answering the below three questions:

  1. Background/Experience in Environmental or Geo-science field
  2. Speak to a project or deal that you were able to work out because of a networking connection through AWG/FWEF/or another organization
  3. Share something interesting about yourself that no one would guess

 

Location: Macalester College, Weyerhaeuser Board Room and Lounge, Weyerhaeuser Hall 

RSVP: by Monday, April 27th, 2009. 

Bus to the Event: Please consider taking public transit to the event. You can find a bus route by using the Metro Transit online trip planner.

Next Forum events are:

  • May 27, 2009 (Integrated Pest Management/Rain Gardens)
  • July 22, 2009 (Annual Outdoor Family Event)
  • September 23, 2009 (Career Panel/Networking)
  • November 18, 2009 (Political Panel)


Men are ALWAYS welcome to every event-please join us!

Price: $0.00
