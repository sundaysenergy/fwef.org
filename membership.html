Become a Member of the Forum!

Consider becoming a new member to the Forum or renew your membership!

Members of The Forum earn many benefits in addition to being part of an amazing community. Dues are paid annually and membership is open to all involved in environmental endeavors.

New member policy!  Membership will now be a one year span from date of purchase, so sign up today for your annual membership!  Add a "membership" to your cart (or use the event and membership option) and complete the checkout process to confirm your registration.  You can pay be credit card through PayPal, or you can mail a check to The Forum, PO Box 14555, Minneapolis, MN  55414.

Benefits include:

  • Receive a discount for each event
  • Additional networking connection
  • A cloth bag (recycled plastic) with the FWEF logo on it!  These are perfect to use as a lunch bag.  Ask for your "member" bag at the next FWEF event.

Students can attend events at the membership rate without being a member.

Become a member today!  Questions about membership? Please contact us!
