About FWEF

The Forum of Women in the Environmental Field is a not-for-profit professional organization, both public and private sectors primarily in Minnesota, focused on building personal and professional networks and promoting a better understanding of our individual and collective roles in preserving and improving the environment. Since 1989!

The Forum of Women in the Environmental Field:
  • Provides current information on environmental topics,
  • Builds personal and professional networks,
  • Encourages and supports women, and
  • Promotes a better understanding of an individual's role in preserving and improving the environment.

The Forum sponsors six events a year on topics related to the environment or environmental careers. Recent topics have included:

  • Women's role in politics
  • Media coverage of environmental issues
  • Art and social change
  • Career strategies for women

Speakers are drawn from a pool of local and national figures working in a variety of environmentally-related fields.
