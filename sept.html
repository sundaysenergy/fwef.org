Autumn Networking Event

Date: 
Jan 23 2013 - 5:30pm - 8:30pm

Flannery Construction
1375 Saint Anthony Ave, Saint Paul, MN 55104

October 3, 2007
7:30 a.m. - 9:00 a.m.

Join us for a breakfast networking event! Get to know other
environmental professionals and talk about current issues. Due to the
popularity of the May networking event we created another event just for
networking. We will have icebreakers and games to facilitate
conversations with other attendees. A continental
breakfast will be provided.


Location: Flannery Construction is located on the northwest corner of I-94 and Hamline Ave at 1375 St. Anthony Ave. It is easily accessible by bus. Please consider using public transit or carpooling. Visit the MTC Web site for bus route and bus schedule information.

Costs: $12 for members and students, $18 for non-members.

RSVP: By Friday September 28, 2007.  Please add this event to your cart and complete the checkout process to confirm your registration. You may choose to pay by credit card or check on the checkout screen.

Price: $18.00
