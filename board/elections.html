2008 Board Member Elections

Just a reminder to send in your applications to become a board member! Here is your chance to be a part of a wonderful team and lead the forum in new endeavors! Board members gain practical experience in planning and implementing programs, learn from each other, and grow from what it takes to maintain a valuable professional organization. There are also many opportunities to network and get involved in whatever your area of interest may be.

If you are interested in running for a position, please submit a short biography by December 20th to Melissa Wenzel.

Your biography should include:

1) Your organization affiliation
2) What you can offer the Board and the Forum (experience, strengths, etc.)

Applications must be submitted by December 20, 2007 and elections will be held in January 2008. If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact us. The Forum is an equal opportunity organization and encourages applicants from all backgrounds.

Thank you and good luck!
